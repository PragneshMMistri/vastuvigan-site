Services

Vasthu Jyothisham Services for Kerala Homes

Structured consultations that guide your home, plot, counselling and existing building decisions with traditional clarity.

Our consultancy services are designed to support every stage of your planning, from plot selection and home layout to complete building review and traditional counselling.

Home Vasthu Consultation

We study your house plans and advise on bedroom placement, kitchen direction, entrance alignment, and living spaces so your home feels balanced and comfortable.

  • Overview: Room planning with direction-based balance.
  • Benefits: Improved well-being, ventilation and flow.
  • Process: Review plans, recommend adjustments, confirm results.

Plot Consultation

Our plot service assesses boundary shapes, road access, slope and orientation to help you choose the best layout for your future building.

  • Overview: Plot analysis for site alignment.
  • Benefits: Smarter planning before construction begins.
  • Process: Share plot details, receive clear analysis.

Vasthu Jyothisham Consultation

This service adds traditional Jyothisham insight to Vasthu advice, helping you plan with cultural depth and practical guidance.

  • Overview: Traditional directional and planning guidance.
  • Benefits: Respectful, informative recommendations.
  • Process: Discuss priorities, receive advice and next steps.

Traditional Counselling

Use this service when you need thoughtful guidance on home decisions, room use, layout changes, or lifestyle planning connected to space.

  • Overview: Counselling grounded in Vasthu and Jyothisham.
  • Benefits: Practical support for important decisions.
  • Process: Consultation, written summary, action suggestions.

Existing Building Review

We review current buildings and recommend feasible Vasthu improvements for better energy, comfort and structure.

  • Overview: Existing home review and improvement plan.
  • Benefits: Better living conditions with minimal change.
  • Process: Analyze existing layout, suggest practical corrections.
