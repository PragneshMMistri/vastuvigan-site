About Us

Our Journey in Traditional Vasthu Jyothisham

VASTHU VIGJAN was established to provide consultation and research in traditional Vasthu and Jyothisham practices with a strong Kerala identity.

Our Journey

VASTHU VIGJAN began with a mission to help families, builders and home owners apply the principles of Vasthu Jyothisham in a clear, respectful and practical way. Our centre is dedicated to research, client-focused counselling and traditional planning practices.

We are based in Erattupetta, Pala. We support clients across the region with plot reviews, home consultations, traditional counselling, and existing building assessments.

— Shri Hari Prasad, Lead Consultant

What is Vasthu Jyothisham?

Traditional Concepts with Informative Clarity

Vasthu Jyothisham combines spatial planning, directional balance, and traditional life guidance in a way that is both practical and culturally rooted.

Vasthu Jyothisham encourages homes and buildings to be arranged in harmony with natural forces, sun direction, wind patterns and the elements. We explain these principles using neutral, informative language so you can understand the benefits without confusion.

The goal is to create spaces that feel balanced, comfortable and supportive for the people who live there. This includes thoughtful room placement, proper direction of kitchens and entrances, and guidance for existing building improvements.
